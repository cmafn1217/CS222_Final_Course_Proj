Reddit

Reddit: r/buildapc

Reddit is a community-based website that lets others connect using different topics put in places called subreddits. Building computers sure does have it's own community on reddit as well. Here at r/buildapc you can seek for help or look into other discussions with people from a similar perspective as you. Whether you are a newbie to PC building or a well-experienced builder, this reddit with over 7 Million members has discussions and topics to offer to both worlds.

PC PartPicker

PC PartPicker

PCPartPicker is a great website for beginners building a PC because it simplifies the process of selecting compatible components. It allows users to easily compare prices across different retailers, check for compatibility between parts, and access community-generated builds and reviews. This helps beginners avoid common mistakes, ensuring they pick the right components that work well together. Additionally, PCPartPicker provides useful tools like part lists, build guides, and a price tracking feature, making it an all-in-one resource for planning and purchasing PC parts.
