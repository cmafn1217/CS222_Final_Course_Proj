What We Offer:

Chatting

Looking to chat about PC's, games, and more? Well you can join the the subreddit r/PcPME to connect with others to have those kinds of conversations! It offers topics related towards PC building, whether it may be prices or a change in components / combos. Additionally, you may also add some feedback of the website to let us know what we can do better to help many more beginners. Questions from others and yourself will be answered by our knowledgable staff, armed with the ability to help you get a deeper understanding of what you need to know without making it too complicated.

Consultation

For a more serious kind of chatting, you are given the opportunity to book one-on-one meetings with staff to guide and/or help you with your journey in PC building. Booking is made easy just by filling out a google form, and giving it around 1-2 business days to get a reply and desired meeting date. Linked below is the google form to request for one-on-one meetings.

Building

If you're feeling scared to handle the somewhat fragile PC components, or frightened to pick out the wrong parts, then fear no longer as our team is willing to do all that for you. All we ask is that you provide a budget, provide what you wish to do with the PC, and any other requests you may have in the google form provided below. Payment for the build will be processed after we give you a list of the parts and see if they meet expectations. This is also a way to give you the right components to purchase, but build it yourself.
