PC Parts List & Low-High Range Combos

Part Description Part Image
CPU A CPU (Central Processing Unit) is the brain of a computer that processes instructions, performs calculations, and manages data to run programs and complete tasks.
GPU A GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) is a part of a computer that focuses on rendering images, videos, and animations. Itâ€™s essential for gaming, video editing, and tasks requiring visual processing.
Motherboard A motherboard is the main circuit board that connects all the components of a computer, such as the CPU, RAM, and storage. It acts as a communication hub, providing power and enabling data transfer between parts to ensure the system functions as a whole.
Memory (RAM) RAM (Random Access Memory) is the short-term memory of a computer, temporarily storing data that the CPU needs to access quickly. It plays a significant role in multitasking and overall system speed by reducing load times and improving responsiveness.
Storage Storage refers to the device where data, programs, and the operating system are saved. It can be a traditional HDD (Hard Disk Drive) or a faster SSD (Solid-State Drive), with SSDs offering quicker access to files and smoother system performance.
Case A PC case is the enclosure that holds and protects all the internal components of a computer. It also helps with cable management and provides mounting points for hardware like the motherboard and storage drives. Proper airflow within the case is crucial to keep components cool, prevent overheating, and ensure stable performance, especially during heavy use.
Fans PC fans are cooling devices used to maintain optimal temperatures inside the computer. They help circulate air, prevent overheating, and ensure that components like the CPU and GPU can operate efficiently during intensive tasks.

Power Supply A PSU (Power Supply Unit) converts electricity from your wall outlet into a usable form for your computer's components. It provides consistent power and determines how many and what types of hardware the system can support.
