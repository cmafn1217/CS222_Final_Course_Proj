PC Components Combinations

This page has PC Component Combinations based on budget and performance. If your budget is low, your performance will follow suit. The Mid Range Combo(s) are usually what's aimed for when building a PC. Together with the combos are links to the products.

Dislcaimer: The prices / parts seen in the combos are not "static" prices. The PC marketprices are always jumping from high to low, but the prices usually stay with the price stated +/-$30-$40. If the combos are not considered low they will be changed accordingly, and more will be added soon.

To see what other componenets fit great with these combos, head to the PC Parts and Links page to get a good understanding of each component, and why they fit well. To go to the PC parts and Links page, just click the respective link in the sidebar.

Low Range Combo

Intel Core i5-12400F & GIGABYTE B760 DS3H

The Intel Core i5-12400F is a budget-friendly processor with 6 cores and 12 threads, offering solid performance for everyday tasks like web browsing, content creation, and 1080p gaming. While lacking integrated graphics, it pairs well with a dedicated GPU for gaming or work builds. When combined with the GIGABYTE B760 DS3H motherboard, the i5-12400F benefits from stable performance and compatibility, making it an excellent choice for a cost-effective, reliable PC build. The B760 DS3H's solid features and efficient power delivery complement the i5-12400F for a smooth user experience.

Mid Range Combo

AMD Ryzen 7 5800X3D & GIGABYTE X570 Aorus Pro

The AMD Ryzen 7 5800X3D, paired with the GIGABYTE X570 Aorus Pro, offers top-tier performance for demanding tasks like gaming and content creation. With 8 cores and 16 threads, the Ryzen 7 5800X3D utilizes 3D V-Cache technology, providing a significant boost in gaming performance, especially in CPU-intensive titles. The X570 Aorus Pro motherboard ensures stability and compatibility, featuring PCIe 4.0 support, robust power delivery, and future-proofing for next-gen components. This combination is perfect for gamers and professionals seeking high performance and reliability in their PC builds.

High Range Combo

AMD Ryzen 9 9950X & Asus X670E Crosshair Hero

The AMD Ryzen 9 9950X is a powerhouse processor with 16 cores and 32 threads, designed for demanding tasks like high-end gaming, video editing, and content creation. It delivers exceptional multi-core performance, making it ideal for users who need top-tier processing power. Paired with the Asus X670E Crosshair Hero motherboard, it forms a high-performance combo that offers excellent stability, superior overclocking capabilities, and cutting-edge PCIe 5.0 support. This combination ensures fast, reliable performance for both work and play, providing an excellent foundation for a premium gaming or professional PC build.
